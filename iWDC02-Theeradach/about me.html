GET TO KNOW MORE ABOUT ME?

  As I mentioned previously about website I started learning photography 4 years ago from scratch. I have always been interested in photography even though I studied computer engineering. Anyway, I was born in Phitsanulok, North part of Thailand where natural resources and fresh air still remain. I live in a small villege in Phitsanulok where I grew up with my family. I love country side very much but however I moved to the big city in order to study at Rajamangala University of technology (RMUTT) and of course to chase for my dream.


  Photography has always been a big part in my youth and I identify myself as a photographer and visual storyteller. Thankfully, my family and friends always support my in anything they see me focusing on. At the beginning I was too focused on getting the perfect and super well executed image but I later realized that all of that doesn’t matter really, its all about the subject and the story you want to tell. This really became apparent when I needed to select the right images to show alongside my writing and both gradually fused together.

  As a photographer who is improving skills in photography. I believe that every seconds is too worthy to waste it without doing nothing. I love to keep happy memories of people. (my own happiness also) One day when I look back and see the photo I had taken. I will realize how happy I were at that moment and right at this present, I must be more focused and happy.

A good photograph represents what we clearly want to express
- Theeradach Subsin-

WHAT MAKE THIS WEBSITE DIFFERENT?

Sure there are thousands of photography websites out there. So you may ask what’s so different about this website? Well the simple (and complicated) answer is nothing and yet everything. We’re unique in our own way.

You could say I am something in between and am not most of me the same way? So this website is for everyone like us – who want to try new things, learning new technique, explore new places and have unforgettable experiences.

Although I will share travel stories from wherever I go, we’re mostly also here to give our expertise on fashion and graduation photography. Working on this photography path gave us a lot of experience, deep knowledge and pulse for the city. I’ll be able to give you exclusive and unique perspectives.

Be confident to walk on your own path,
it's the only thing that makes you different
- Theeradach Subsin-
