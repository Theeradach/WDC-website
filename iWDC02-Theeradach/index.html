SAMET ISLAND

The sun slowly faded away I sat right there to hear the wave and waited for the sea wind to impact my face. So so nice day. Anyway, It was time to have dinner, not only because it was dark but our stomachs demanded it.

KOH SAMET FERRY

There is no such other way to visit Samet island without taking a Boat, you probably swim to get there if you dare to race with the sharks. Boat trips are the best way to visit all the hidden beaches and coves on Koh Samet.

KANOM BEACH

The beaches here are less crowded and it is a place you go to either unwind and relax or for amazing adventures. Of course it should definetely be on your bucket list to visit in Thailand.

SI CHANG ISLAND

To get away from the chaos of BKK this is one of my favorite island. A very local island with villages and slow , peaceful life. The island is only 8 sq km, very small island but it is perfect for a day worth of exploring.

KHIRI, CHONBURI

Grand conyond chonburi or snow mountain, whatever. This time, we've made it a point to go outside of BKK. I walked up to the pile of rocks and it looked like I was on the snow hills or something.

BLACK SEA

Photo can't even describe how real beautiful this black sea is. Black sand grains will appeal you not to leave this place as if being spelled by black magic.
